Who am I?

As a Senior Data Analyst with over 4 years of experience in the field, I have a strong background in data analysis and visualization, as well as a track record of delivering actionable insights and customized reports. I am skilled in SQL, Python, and visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau and Looker).

Throughout my career, I have worked with a variety of clients and companies, including Warren Brasil, Dell Technologies, and Sentimonitor, where I have gained experience in geospatial analysis, web scraping, data modeling, data mining, tracking strategy implementation, financial dashboarding, and consulting for social media agencies.

In addition to my professional experience, I am currently pursuing a MicroMaster in Statistics and Data Science from MITx and have completed the IBM Data Science Professional Certificate through Coursera. I am fluent in English and Spanish and hold a degree in Economics from the University of Rio Grande do Sul.
